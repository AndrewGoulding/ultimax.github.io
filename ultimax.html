Ultimax: Crossover! is a turn-based role-playing game with characters from many different franchises, including Mario, Zelda, Pok√©mon, Dragon Ball, One piece, and Yu-Gi-Oh. You can create your character with customization options from the many different franchises.

The story starts with your created character at a battle academy. This academy teaches courses in many different schools of battle. Your character is tasked with going back to the past to become the ultimate duelist. However, it will not be easy, as an evil organization known as the time-splitters are trying to take over the world with power from the past!

The characters in the game have the following statistics:

Health: Determines how much damage can be taken before the character dies.
Strength: Determines how much damage your physical attacks deal to the opponent.
Defence: Determines how much damage physical attacks deal to you.
Intelligence: Determines how much damage magical attacks deal to the opponent.
Willpower: Determines how much damage magical attacks deal to you.
Dexteriy: Determines turn order.

The following status effects are in the game:

Poision: Associated with the venom element. Causes 2.5% HP damage per turn which increases by 2.5% per turn.
Sleep: The character cannot act in battle for 3 turns or until hit by a physical attack.
Freeze: Associated with the ice element. Cannot act in battle for 3 turns or until hit by a fire elemental attack.
Paralysis: Associated with the electric element. 50% chance to not be able to act on turn order, and lowers the dexterity stat.
Blind: Associated with the light element. 50% chance to miss your move, and lowers your willpower stat.
Confusion: Associated with the dark element. 25% chance to not be able to act on turn order and 25% chance to hit a friendly party member, lowers your intelligence stat.
Rooted: Associated with the grass element. You cannot switch this party member and causes 5% HP damage per turn which is then healed to the opponent.
Burn: Asscoiated with the fire element. Causes 5% HP damage over time and lowers your strength stat.
